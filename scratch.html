US National Parks Road Trip

From February 13 to February 16, 2026, I took a four-day road trip focused on exploring several iconic national parks in the region.

Trip Highlights

  1. Zion National Park
    • Kicked off the trip in Provo
    • Took in the massive sandstone cliffs
  2. Bryce Canyon
    • Saw the famous hoodoos
    • Enjoyed high-altitude views
  3. Capitol Reef
    • Drove through the Waterpocket Fold
    • Visited the historic orchards of Fruita

I also wrapped up the park circuit in Moab, visiting the red rock arches and the vast overlooks of the Island in the Sky district at Arches & Canyonlands.
